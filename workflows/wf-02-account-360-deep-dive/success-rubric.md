# Success Rubric: Account 360 Deep Dive

**Workflow:** WF-02 | **Mode:** Co-Work | **Actor:** CSA

## Expected Output

Comprehensive account narrative with health assessment, opportunity landscape, whitespace hypotheses, stakeholder map status, recommended next actions

## Routing Requirements

- **Entry skill:** portfolio-account-planning-coach
- **Modules:** account-review, whitespace-growth-hypotheses
- **Conditional handoffs:**
  - acr-signal-to-opportunity-coach (when: ACR signals warrant investigation)
  - customer-meeting-prep-outreach-coach (when: stakeholder engagement needed)

## Quality Gates

- Output must be actionable (named owners, specific next steps)
- Evidence must be cited with freshness labels
- No fabricated data points
- Routing must match contract unless scenario is design-redesign intent
- Guardrails respected (draft-only, no unauthorized writeback)
