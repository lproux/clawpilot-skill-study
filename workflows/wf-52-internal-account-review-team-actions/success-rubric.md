# Success Rubric: Internal Account Review Transcript -> Team Actions

**Workflow:** WF-52 | **Mode:** Co-Work | **Actor:** Manager

## Expected Output

Team action items with owners, coaching notes (constructive frame), customer-facing draft (sanitized), manager decision items, MSX update proposals, follow-up cadence

## Routing Requirements

- **Entry skill:** customer-meeting-prep-outreach-coach
- **Modules:** follow-up-and-decisions
- **Conditional handoffs:**
  - forecast-business-review-coach (when: team coaching or risk review input)
  - pipeline-msx-hygiene-coach (when: MSX updates warranted from review)

## Quality Gates

- Output must be actionable (named owners, specific next steps)
- Evidence must be cited with freshness labels
- No fabricated data points
- Routing must match contract unless scenario is design-redesign intent
- Guardrails respected (draft-only, no unauthorized writeback)
