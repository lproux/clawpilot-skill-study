# Success Rubric: Post-Meeting Follow-Up

**Workflow:** WF-12 | **Mode:** Co-Work | **Actor:** CSA/DSSP

## Expected Output

Decision log, action items with owners and due dates, customer-facing follow-up email draft, MSX writeback proposals

## Routing Requirements

- **Entry skill:** customer-meeting-prep-outreach-coach
- **Modules:** follow-up-and-decisions, email-chat-context
- **Conditional handoffs:**
  - pipeline-msx-hygiene-coach (when: MSX updates warranted from meeting outcomes)

## Quality Gates

- Output must be actionable (named owners, specific next steps)
- Evidence must be cited with freshness labels
- No fabricated data points
- Routing must match contract unless scenario is design-redesign intent
- Guardrails respected (draft-only, no unauthorized writeback)
